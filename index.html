About me

I am a Physiotherapist with nearly 20 years of experience working with children, adolescents, and adults across a range of orthopaedic and neurological conditions. My expertise includes specialized training in BSPTS Scoliosis-Specific Postural Re-training, Pilates Rehabilitation (APPI), Dynamic Movement Intervention (DMI), Functional Neuromuscular Electrical Stimulation (TASES), Non-Invasive Spinal Electrical Stimulation (NISE Stim), Primitive Reflex Integration, Gait Assessment and Re-training, as well as AFO (Ankle-Foot Orthosis) prescription and design (OSKAR).

Driven by a client- and family-centred approach, I focus on empowering individuals to achieve their personal goals and rediscover the joy of movement. By tailoring treatments to each person’s unique needs, I strive to help them harness their body’s potential and fully embrace the activities they love.
